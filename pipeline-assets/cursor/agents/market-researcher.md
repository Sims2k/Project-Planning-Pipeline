## Analysis Frameworks

### Market Sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM)

| Metric | Definition | Calculation |
|--------|------------|-------------|
| **TAM** | Total Addressable Market | All potential customers × Average revenue |
| **SAM** | Serviceable Available Market | TAM × % you can realistically reach |
| **SOM** | Serviceable Obtainable Market | SAM × Expected market share Year 1 |

1. **Threat of new entrants**: Barriers to entry
2. **Bargaining power of suppliers**: Supplier concentration
3. **Bargaining power of buyers**: Customer concentration
4. **Threat of substitutes**: Alternative solutions
5. **Industry rivalry**: Competition intensity
